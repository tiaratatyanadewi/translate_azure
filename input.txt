# XYZ PHARMACEUTICALS  
## PRODUCT DOSSIER: RESPIREASE COUGH SYRUP  

**Product Name:** RespiRease Cough Syrup  
**Dosage Form:** Oral Liquid Syrup  
**Manufacturer:** XYZ Pharmaceuticals Ltd.  
**Strength:** 15 mg/5 mL Dextromethorphan HBr, 100 mg/5 mL Guaifenesin  

## COMPOSITION  
**Each 5 mL contains:**  
- Dextromethorphan Hydrobromide USP: 15 mg  
- Guaifenesin USP: 100 mg  
- **Excipients:** Sucrose, Glycerin, Sodium Benzoate, Natural Cherry Flavor, Citric Acid, Purified Water  

## THERAPEUTIC CATEGORY  
Antitussive and Expectorant  

## PHARMACOLOGICAL ACTION  
**Dextromethorphan HBr:** Centrally acting cough suppressant that affects the cough center in the medulla oblongata.  
**Guaifenesin:** Expectorant that helps loosen congestion in the chest and throat by increasing respiratory tract fluid secretions.  

## INDICATION  
For the temporary relief of cough due to minor throat and bronchial irritation associated with the common cold, and to help loosen phlegm and thin bronchial secretions to make coughs more productive.  

## DOSAGE AND ADMINISTRATION  
**Adults and children 12 years and over:**  
- 10 mL (2 teaspoons) every 4 hours  
- Do not exceed 6 doses (60 mL) in 24 hours  

**Children 6 to under 12 years:**  
- 5 mL (1 teaspoon) every 4 hours  
- Do not exceed 6 doses (30 mL) in 24 hours  

**Children under 6 years:** Not recommended  

## CONTRAINDICATIONS  
- Hypersensitivity to any of the ingredients  
- Patients taking MAO inhibitors or within 14 days of stopping such treatment  
- Patients with chronic productive cough  

## WARNINGS AND PRECAUTIONS  
- Not for persistent or chronic cough  
- Consult a healthcare professional if cough persists for more than 7 days, comes back, or is accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache  
- May cause drowsiness; alcohol may intensify this effect  
- Use caution when driving or operating machinery  
- Not recommended for children under 6 years of age  

## SIDE EFFECTS  
Common side effects include:  
- Nausea  
- Drowsiness  
- Dizziness  
- Mild stomach discomfort
